Our Story

Crumb & Co. started in 2019 in a small home kitchen, with one mixing bowl and a family chocolate chip recipe passed down three generations.

Every cookie is still shaped, baked, and packed by hand in small batches — no preservatives, no shortcuts. Just real butter, good chocolate, and a lot of taste-testing.
